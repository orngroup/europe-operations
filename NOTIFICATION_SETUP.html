CapEye — Notification Setup

Two parts: Email (15 mins) + PWA Push (10 mins) · Both are free
1 Create a Free EmailJS Account
  • Go to emailjs.com and click Sign Up Free
  • Use your Auto Capital email — the free plan allows 200 emails/month which is plenty
  • Verify your email address when prompted
2 Connect Your Email (Gmail works fine)
  • In the EmailJS dashboard, click Email Services → Add New Service
  • Choose Gmail (or Outlook if you prefer)
  • Name it exactly: service_capeye
  • Click Connect Account and log in with your Auto Capital Google account
  • Click Create Service — note the Service ID shown (it should be service_capeye)
3 Create the Email Template
  • Click Email Templates → Create New Template
  • Name it: CapEye Notification
  • Set Template ID to exactly: template_capeye
  • In the To Email field type: {{to_email}}
  • In the Subject field type: {{subject}}
  • In the Content area — switch to HTML mode and paste: {{html_body}}
  • Click Save
The {{variable}} placeholders are automatically filled in by CapEye when it sends a notification.
4 Get Your Public Key
  • Click your account name (top right) → Account
  • Under API Keys, copy your Public Key (looks like: abc123XYZdef456)
5 Update notifications.js

Open notifications.js in a text editor and change these 3 lines at the top:

// BEFORE (lines 9-11): var EMAILJS_SERVICE_ID = 'service_capeye'; var EMAILJS_TEMPLATE_ID = 'template_capeye'; var EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY = 'YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY'; ← paste your key here var EMAILJS_ENABLED = false; ← change this to true

Upload notifications.js to GitHub. Emails will now send on every handover.

✓ Email notifications are now live. Test by doing a handover in workflow — the recipient will get an email within 30 seconds.
6 Enable PWA Push Notifications (Phone Lock Screen)
  • Go to console.firebase.google.com → your capeye-autocapital project
  • Click the ⚙️ gear → Project Settings
  • Click the Cloud Messaging tab
  • Scroll to Web Push certificates → click Generate key pair
  • Copy the Key pair value shown (long string starting with B...)
7 Add VAPID Key to notifications.js

In notifications.js, update line 16:

// Change this: var VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY = 'YOUR_VAPID_KEY'; var PUSH_ENABLED = false; // To this: var VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY = 'Bxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx'; var PUSH_ENABLED = true;

Upload notifications.js to GitHub.

8 Staff Install on Phone (Add to Home Screen)
  • Android: Open Chrome → visit the CapEye URL → tap the 3-dot menu → Add to Home Screen → Install. The Auto Capital icon will appear on their home screen.
  • iPhone: Open Safari → visit the CapEye URL → tap the Share button (box with arrow) → Add to Home Screen → Add.
  • First time they open it, they'll see a browser prompt: "Allow CapEye to send notifications?" — they must tap Allow
  • After that, they'll get a notification on their lock screen whenever a vehicle is handed to them — just like WhatsApp
✓ Once installed and allowed, staff get instant lock-screen notifications on handover — no WhatsApp, no app store, completely free.
